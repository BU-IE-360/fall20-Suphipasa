4.5G and 3G trends

What is done?

My purpose was observing 4.5G and 3G user count and connecting that data to Google “4.5G” search data. 4.5G and 3G user counts are inverse, people abandon 3G and start to use 4.5G. Trend data and new user data are resembling each other, people search before start to use 4.5G or abandon 3G. Interesting part is although 3G abandonment continue, interest and newcomers to 4.5G and 3G abandonment is decreasing over time.

Netflix Stock Price

We all used and still using Netflix during Pandemic. I wanted to see stock prices of Netflix before and at beginning of the Pandemic. Compared NFLX stock prices 2016-2019 to trend data of “NFLX” and “Netflix” searches. There is no certain trend on NFLX prices but there is an overall rise. When we look at the search data “Netflix” searches are more resembles than “NFLX” searches, the reason behind is Netflix is a service platform and its popularity and “brand power” determines the success.
